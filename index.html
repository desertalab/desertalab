How it works

Three simple steps.

No hopping from shop to shop. Start with what you want, and let LAONI do the running around.

1

Tell us what you need

Search by product or just say it — "2kg rice, a notebook, cooking oil." No need to pick a shop first.

2

3

It arrives fast

Order in a tap, pay by UPI, and get it delivered from just around the corner.

FAQ

Good to know.

Is LAONI available now?

Not yet — we're preparing to launch in Mysuru. Join the early-access list and we'll let you know the moment it opens in your area.

Which areas will you cover?

We're starting in Mysuru, neighbourhood by neighbourhood — Gokulam, Vijayanagar, Kuvempunagar and more. Early sign-ups help us decide where to launch first.

How will I pay?

Simple UPI payment at checkout, once the app is live.

Are these really local shops?

Yes. LAONI connects you to genuine neighbourhood merchants near you — the same shops you already pass every day.

I own a shop — how do I join?

Register your store below, or take the 3-minute merchant survey. We'll get in touch about being one of the first shops on LAONI.
