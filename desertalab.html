About Deserta Lab

One company, building for local commerce.

“The shops around you should be as easy to discover as anything online.”

Deserta Lab is a small studio building technology for the way commerce really works in Indian neighbourhoods. We believe the corner shop deserves the same great tools as the biggest brands — and that local businesses should be empowered, not replaced.

LAONI is our first product, and we're starting close to home in Mysuru, with the shops and streets that make a city feel like home. Everything we build going forward lives under Deserta Lab.

FAQ

Good to know.

What is Deserta Lab?

Deserta Lab is the company building technology for local commerce. LAONI is our first product — a way to discover and order from the shops near you.

And what is LAONI?

LAONI lets you search for what you need and find it in stock in shops around you, then get it delivered. Instead of picking a store first, you start from the product.

Is LAONI available yet?

Not quite — we're launching soon, starting in Mysuru. Join early access and we'll let you know the moment it opens in your area.

Will it cost me anything to use?

LAONI is free to join and browse. We'll share full details with our early-access members before launch.

I run a shop — how do I join?

Register your shop on this page, or take the short merchant survey. Both help us bring LAONI to your neighbourhood and build it around what shops like yours actually need.

How is my information used?

Your details are used only to keep you updated about LAONI's launch and — for shops — the merchant pilot. Nothing else.
